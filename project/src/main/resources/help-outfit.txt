SEEING WHAT YOU WEAR
---------------------
outfit (to see a short quick summary of what you are wearing)
outfit detailed (to see more details about what you are wearing)

The detailed outfit description shows the quality of the items,
the average quality of them and the estimated coverage of you body.
Quality and coverage are both percentages.
