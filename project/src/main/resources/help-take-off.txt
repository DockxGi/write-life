=================
HELP -> TAKE OFF
=================
See: HELP WEAR to know how to add items to your outfit
See: HELP OUTFIT to see how you can have a look at your current outfit

TAKE OF CLOTHES
----------------
take-off <itemName>
take-off item <positionOfItem> (example: take-off item chest_outer)

If you take off clothes then they will be put in your inventory.
So make sure that you have enough space in your inventory before you take off something.
