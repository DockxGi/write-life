=============
HELP -> WEAR
=============
You can wear some items on your body. When you do this then
the items will be removed from your inventory and will become part of your outfit.

OUTFIT LOCATIONS
-----------------
The type of item will determine where it will be placed on your body.
The possible locations are:

1. head
2. neck
3. hands
4. chest-inner
5. chest-outer
6. legs-inner
7. legs-outer
8. feet

WEARING AN ITEM
----------------
wear <itemName>
wear item <positionInListOfItems>

Not all items have a name. When you use the inventory-command then
you will see a list of items. Before each item is a number.
This is the positionInListOfItems.
