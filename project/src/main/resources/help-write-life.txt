===================
HELP -> WRITE LIFE
===================
Write life is a text-based sandbox game.
Think of it as a text-only version of Second Life, but then single player,
does only run/save un your pc and the virtual objects do not cost any real money.

To start you first have to create your virtual world.
The second step is to create your player character.
The third step is to enter your world with the player character.

Once you enter the world with your player character you can interact with it: create new areas,
create new objects, add enemies, puzzles, magic, change the weather and so on.

The goal is to use enjoy your own fantastic imaginary world.

related topics: world, player
